Memorable date in Russian history: May 9, 1945.

Description: May 9, 1945, marks Victory Day in the Great Patriotic War, the conflict between
the USSR and Nazi Germany, which was part of World War II. This day became a symbol of the Soviet
people's victory in the most devastating war in history, resulting in the complete defeat of Nazism.
The Victory Parade on Red Square in Moscow and mass celebrations throughout the country were vivid
expressions of joy and pride in the victory, and the memory of those events remains alive
and important to the Russian people.
